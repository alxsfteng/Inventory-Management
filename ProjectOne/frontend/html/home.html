Welcome to Inventory Management

This application helps you manage your inventory efficiently. You can add, edit, and delete items and warehouses, while keeping track of stock levels and locations.

How it Works

To get started, click on the "Warehouses" tab above to view and manage your warehouses and items. You can add new items, update existing ones, and remove items as needed.

Feel free to explore the features and functionalities provided by the application to streamline your inventory management processes.
